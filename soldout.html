Sol Abstraction is an interative, generative art project built on Solana, and aimed at creating collaboration between community and AI, based on the souls of humankind.

Profile Images, discussion and feedback from our community will be combined and distorted through GAN and ANN models to create completely new, unique pieces of abstract art.

From each seed from the AI models, 3 curated pieces of abstract art will be selected, which will consist of a snapshot in time along the generative distortion of the abstract flow.

The seed image is born out of algorithms that have been trained using the humanform inputs from our community. Resemblances to the human shape are still apparent.
